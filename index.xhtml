Downstyle

A tool to fight CSS bloat.

Learn more in the README.

Usage

downstyle!

Just drag the link above to your browser's bookmarks toolbar.
You can then use it to instantly simplify the styling of any page on the web. ⚠
(Hint: click the link to try it on this page!)

✻

Alternative bookmarklets are available for a sans-serif mode,
as well as a reading mode that emulates a "paper sheet" look:

downstyle+sans bookify

✻

Downstyle can also be used as a regular stylesheet.
Download downstyle.css , or include the following code in your HTML: 

<link rel="stylesheet"
href="http://rawgit.com/waldyrious/downstyle/master/downstyle.css" />
